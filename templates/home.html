Sherlock Holmes' Clue Tracker

Step into the shoes of Sherlock Holmes in "The Artwork Heist Unveiled." Engage in a riveting investigation set in the atmospheric backdrop of Victorian London. As Holmes, you meticulously collect clues, from subtle hints to overlooked details, to uncover the truth behind a daring artwork theft. Utilize the web-based "Clue Tracker" to organize and analyze evidence, piecing together the puzzle of the heist. Navigate through a labyrinth of deceit and deception, where every twist and turn leads closer to unraveling the mystery. With keen observation and deductive reasoning, follow Holmes's footsteps to unveil the truth behind the crime.
